about our company

Eco-Friendly Lawn & Landscaping, LLC was founded to develop and expand in response to environmental concerns. We are proud to specialize in some of the newer technologies to cope with some of the sustainable issues such as water pollution, stormwater management, pesticide toxicity, soil health, and fertilizer runoff.

We are a full service, eco friendly, economical, and sustainable landscape company. We are committed to create beautiful and healthy landscapes preserving the earth's resources.
